**Fulcrum**
Most recently I was a founding designer at Fulcrum, where I owned end-to-end product experiences to automate manual workflows for insurance brokerages. In six months, I designed two core workflows, certificates and proposals, built the design system for the chat assistant, designed a control center for accounts, a dashboard for loss runs, and a template builder for document creation.

**Hanomi**
As a founding designer at Hanomi, I operated across product, design, and marketing. I designed an intelligence layer that sits on desktop CAD applications for mechanical engineers, and owned the upload-to-download user flow for our core web platform.

**Decathlon**
I have worked at Decathlon (the largest sporting goods retailer) for a large part of my career, starting as an intern in 2019. At Decathlon UK, I designed the B2B platform, and at Decathlon US I designed the web experience to launch Van Rysel, a French luxury bike brand, to the US market.
