**Fulcrum**
As the founding designer at Fulcrum, I designed end-to-end product experiences to automate manual workflows for insurance brokerages. In six months, I owned the end-to-end user flow for two core workflows: certificates and proposals, built the design system for a chat assistant, designed a control center for accounts, and a dashboard for loss runs.

**Hanomi**
As a founding designer at Hanomi, I designed intelligence layers for mechanical engineers. I owned the upload-to-download user flow for our core web platform, and designed a desktop intelligence layer for Solidworks.

**Decathlon**
I have worked at Decathlon for a large part of my career, starting as an intern in 2019. At Decathlon UK, I designed the B2B platform, and at Decathlon US I designed the web experience for launching Van Rysel, a French luxury bike brand, to the US market.
